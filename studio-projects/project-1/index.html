HELIOPOLIS:
THE ANCIENT EGYPTIAN CREATION MYTH

Instagram

I first re-told this myth through a business Instagram profile with a feed that simulates the landscape of the Earth by using photo grids, and I also featured each of the Gods and Goddesses and describing them as CEOS, heads, and managers of this company.



Google Drawings

I also communicated this myth in the form of a Google Drawing by using shapes and images to create a cohesive picture that features all the landscapes, Gods, Goddesses, symbols, and elements within this story.



Google Docs

Lastly, I explored the idea of modern-day hieroglyphics through the use of emojis, and I achieved this by typing in emojis, images, and symbols into a Google Doc to communicate each sentence of the myth with translations in the comments.
