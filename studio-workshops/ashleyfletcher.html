Mia Gilling, Interviewed by Ashley Fletcher

Mia's Online Habits

Mia likes to watch YouTube while eating. She uses Instagram as her main way to communicate with her friends because she lives in Arizona away from her friends in New York. She also uses Snapchat mainly for the memories feature as well as to chat with a couple of people. When Mia is on Twitter, she likes to mindlessly scroll through the platform. She also recently re-downloaded Bumble where she spent a little bit of time making her profile and exploring the app. Mia loves to take pictures throughout the day, so the camera and photo apps are both heavily used by her.

“My favorite app is Spotify because I constantly need to have music on. I use music when I'm trying to sleep, when I'm cooking, driving, all the time. I also love following my friends and seeing what music they're listening to.”
— Mia Gilling
